Draft Orchestration Playbooks

Three orchestration tracks cover the full 180M addressable audience. Semcasting's identity spine connects each person to the channels they can be reached on — then coordinates frequency and sequencing across Paid Digital and Owned & Operated channels.

⚡ Channel Trigger Logic — How Orchestration Works
✓  IF customer clicks paid digital ad
→ Turn OFF email · suppress further paid impressions · route to SMS booking link
✗  IF customer does NOT click after 3 exposures
→ Trigger email · escalate to push notification · hold CTV for next burst
⚠️  IF started but did not complete scheduling
→ Trigger SMS with direct booking link · escalate display retargeting · suppress CTV to avoid over-exposure
📊  Attribution confirmed for every converted individual
→ Channel-level credit assigned · feeds back into next season's audience model
